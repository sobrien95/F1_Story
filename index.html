Chapter 1

Alain Prost was widely regarded as the man to beat in Formula 1 towards the end of the 1980s.

Chapter 2

Ayrton Senna was a passionate racer from Brazil who was renowned for his daring overtakes and skill in the rain.

Chapter 3

The two drivers locked horns in 1988 and in subsequent years when they were both signed to drive for McLaren Honda, providing Formula 1 fans with some of the greatest battles of the sport's history.
